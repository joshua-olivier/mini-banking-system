Desciption

A banking system is a network of institutions that provide financial services to existing and new bankers. These institutions are responsible for operating a payment system, making loans, accepting deposits and assisting with investments. In this banking system, I included the Chequing, Savings, RESP (Registered Education Services Plan), and the RRSP (Registered Retirement Savings Plan) accounts. I think this tool would be very helpful for all bankers because it reduces the cost of obtaining information about both savings and borrowing opportunities. It makes the overall economy more efficient for them.
